# KPIs & Metrics

## Program Efficacy
- Phish-prone rate; time-to-click; credential submit rate; dwell time.
- Report rate and false-positive rate; repeat offender rate.

## Learning Outcomes
- Microlearning engagement; assessment score lift; completion rates.
- Adaptive difficulty progression and remediation completion.

## Operational Health
- Delivery success; bounce/spam complaint; end-to-end latency; event loss.
- Processing success rate; queue depth; worker retries.

## Security Outcomes
- MTTR for reported phish; % reported vs delivered; closed-loop SIEM coverage.
- Risk score deltas at user/department/org levels; trend analysis.

## Business Reporting
- Departmental heatmaps; quarter-over-quarter improvement; ROI proxies.
- Executive/board-ready report exports and schedule adherence.
