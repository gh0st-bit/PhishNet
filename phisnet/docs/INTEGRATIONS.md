# Ecosystem Integrations

## Identity & Provisioning
- SAML/OIDC providers: Azure AD, Okta, Google Workspace.
- SCIM: automatic user/group provisioning and deprovisioning.

## Messaging & Reporting
- Slack/Teams bots for nudges, reminders, and inline report-phish.
- Outlook/Gmail add-ins for Report-Phish to backend.

## Security & IT
- SIEM forwarders: Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, QRadar (CEF/LEEF/JSON).
- SOAR hooks: Cortex XSOAR, Swimlane; ticketing with Jira/ServiceNow.
- EDR/MDM awareness: surface device posture to simulations.

## Email Delivery
- Gateways: M365, Google; Providers: SES/SendGrid/Postmark.
- Feedback/complaints/bounces webhooks ingested for deliverability analytics.
